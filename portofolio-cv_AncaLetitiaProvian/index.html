About me

I'm Letitia and I'm a

Passionate and proactive Technical Writer Manager with 6 years of experience in content editing and technical documentation, I have developed a passion for technology and the IT industry. A former journalist, content writer, and web editor, I have transitioned from mass media to the software/engineering and financial services industries. My play tools are DITA-authoring, Oxygen XML Author, SDL Publication Manager, Inkscape, Adobe Acrobat, Adobe Illustrator, Visio. I am currently interested in Project Management, Team leading, APIs and coding skills. I like to keep an open mind and be in sync with technological development.
